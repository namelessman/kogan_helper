Welcome! Use this extension to quickly view your mobile plan details and data usage at a glance.

🚀 Features at a Glance

  • ✨ Easily track your Kogan Mobile plan

  • 📅 See your expiration date and usage
  • 🌍 Check your plan from any website
  • 🔔 Get notifications when data is low
  • 🔒 100% local – no data is stored or sent
  • 🛡️ Secure and private – runs in your browser
